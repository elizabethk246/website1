My name is Elizabeth Kennedy, I’m a twenty-year-old philosophy student at Durham University with a lifelong love of art in all its forms. When I was young and people asked what I wanted to be, my answer never wavered: an artist. That hasn't changed and I couldn’t be more excited to be realising that dream, displaying in a local gallery for the first time.

Painting and drawing are where I feel most at home. What draws me to these is the chance to record beautiful things the way my mind's eye sees them; to translate a moment, face, or place into something a little more than the sum of its parts.

Alongside my own work, I take on portrait and painting commissions. There's something I find genuinely special about helping someone capture an idea which matters to them by turning it into something immortalised and entirely unique.
